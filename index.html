How it works

From database to dashboard
in three steps

No setup wizard. No documentation to study. Just connect, ask, and you're done.

Connect your database

Paste a connection string. PostgreSQL, MySQL, BigQuery, Snowflake, or 15+ others. Read-only by default. Takes 30 seconds.

2

Ask your question

Type exactly what you want to know. "Top 10 customers by revenue this quarter." We write the query, run it, and return results instantly.
