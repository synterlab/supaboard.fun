How it works

Three steps to your first dashboard

Go from raw database to shareable chart in under five minutes.

1

Connect your database

Paste a connection string for PostgreSQL, MySQL, BigQuery, Snowflake, or any other source. Takes about 30 seconds.

2

Ask a question

Type what you want to know in plain English. "Show top customers by revenue this quarter." Supaboard writes the query for you.

3

Share or embed

Results come back as a chart or table. Share a link, export to PDF, or embed the dashboard anywhere with one click.
